Admissions

Thank you for your interest in Bishop Dunstan Nsubuga Memorial Secondary School Kalanga. We welcome applications from motivated students eager to learn and grow.

How to Apply

  1. Obtain Application Form: Application forms can be downloaded from our website (link coming soon) or picked up from the school's administrative office during working hours.
  2. Submit Application: Complete the form thoroughly and submit it along with all required documents before the stated deadline.
  3. Entrance Examination/Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an entrance examination and/or interview.
  4. Admission Offer: Successful candidates will receive an admission offer, along with details on fees and enrollment procedures.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copies of previous academic reports/transcripts
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Recommendation letter (if applicable)
