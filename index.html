Admissions Process

Thank you for your interest in our school. We welcome applications from motivated students eager to learn and grow in a dynamic community.

How to Apply

  1. Obtain Application Form: Download from our website (link coming soon) or pick one up from the school's administrative office.
  2. Submit Application: Complete the form and submit it with all required documents before the deadline.
  3. Entrance Examination/Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an entrance assessment.
  4. Admission Offer: Successful candidates receive an admission offer with enrollment details.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copies of previous academic reports
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Passport-sized photographs
